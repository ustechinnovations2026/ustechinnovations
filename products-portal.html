USTECH Portal

Your secure gateway to remote device monitoring, calibration management, data analytics, and real-time spectroscopy insights — all from a single unified dashboard.

What is USTECH Portal?

USTECH Portal is a cloud-based platform that connects your spectroscopy devices, calibration models, and process data into one centralized workspace. Whether you manage a single analyzer or a global fleet, the Portal gives you full visibility and control — accessible from any browser, anywhere.

📊

Remote Monitoring

View real-time measurement data, device health status, and prediction outputs from all connected analyzers through a live dashboard — no VPN or on-site access required.

🔄

Calibration Management

Upload, version, and deploy calibration models to devices remotely. Track model performance over time and push updates across your entire analyzer fleet in seconds.

📈

Data Analytics

Aggregate spectral and prediction data from multiple sites. Generate trend reports, export datasets for offline analysis, and identify process deviations before they impact quality.

Key Features
01

Fleet Management

Register and organize all your USTECH analyzers in one place. Monitor connectivity, firmware versions, and operational status across multiple production sites and facilities.

02

Secure Access Control

Role-based permissions ensure that operators, engineers, and managers see exactly what they need. Multi-factor authentication and encrypted data transfer protect your proprietary process information.

03

Automated Alerts & Notifications

Configure threshold-based alerts for any measured parameter. Receive instant email or SMS notifications when a device goes offline, a prediction exceeds limits, or maintenance is due.

04

Historical Data & Audit Trail

Access complete measurement history with full traceability. Every calibration change, prediction result, and device event is logged for compliance reporting and continuous improvement.

How It Works

Getting started with USTECH Portal is simple. Connect your devices and start gaining insights within minutes.

1

Connect

Register your USTECH analyzers via ethernet or Wi-Fi. Each device automatically syncs with the Portal upon first connection.

2

Monitor

Access real-time dashboards showing live measurements, device health, and prediction outputs across your entire fleet.

3

Optimize

Use built-in analytics to identify trends, deploy updated calibrations remotely, and drive continuous process improvement.
