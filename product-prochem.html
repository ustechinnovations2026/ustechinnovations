Introduction

Every Minute a Batch Waits is a Minute of Margin Lost

Every minute a batch waits on a lab result is a minute of margin lost. ProChem was engineered to close that gap. Developed by USTECH, it is a powerful FT-NIR analysis software designed to optimize complex measurement and analysis processes that run through laboratories and production lines alike.

From incoming raw materials to semi-finished goods and final products, ProChem brings every critical quality checkpoint under one intelligent system—eliminating the time and labor losses of traditional analysis methods and replacing them with flawless, real-time process control.

Complete Ecosystem Integration

Paired with USTECH's ProLine2550 FT-NIR Analyzers, ProChem combines laboratory-level accuracy with production-line speed, giving you one unified platform for configuration, monitoring, and immediate decision-making.

System Integration

How It Fits Into Your System

ProChem is the central software platform for NIR spectroscopy, seamlessly connecting with compatible analytical instruments for fast, secure, and reliable data acquisition and analysis.

Its modular architecture enables users to manage multiple product profiles, analytical methods, and calibration models within a single platform, ensuring flexibility as production requirements and product portfolios evolve.

🔌 Plant-Wide Automation

ProChem integrates seamlessly with plant automation systems, communicating securely with PLCs through a robust Ethernet-based HTTP interface. The ProChem Automation module enables remote control of product selection and analytical methods directly from the PLC, while delivering real-time analytical results back to the control system.

This allows operators to monitor product quality continuously and respond immediately to process changes, improving efficiency, consistency, and production control.

COMPANION MODULE

Review Measurements Without Interruption

ProChem Viewer provides secure, read-only access to live and historical analytical data through a dedicated monitoring interface. As ProChem continues running measurements, authorized users can simultaneously monitor live results, review historical data, and evaluate process performance without interrupting the ongoing analysis.

Whether accessed from the same workstation or a connected computer, ProChem Viewer delivers complete visibility while keeping all measurement settings and operational controls exclusively in the hands of the ProChem operator.

Clear Separation of Responsibilities

ProChem and ProChem Viewer work together to provide a secure and efficient analytical workflow. Measurement control remains exclusively within ProChem, while ProChem Viewer delivers real-time access to analytical results and spectral data for authorized users.

Designed for laboratories and industrial environments alike, the system enables multiple users to follow analytical processes simultaneously without affecting active measurements, ensuring uninterrupted operation and reliable process visibility.

Key Benefits of ProChem Viewer

Dual-Screen Monitoring

Reflects data to the screen without delay the instant a measurement is running in the main application—giving operations the comfort of two synchronized windows.

Control Boundary

No new calibration or measurement can be started from the Viewer. This deliberate design choice protects the integrity of the main system while keeping monitoring secure.

Graph & Curve Tracking

Fully synchronized with the main system, spectral curves and graphical changes generated during measurement can be followed instantly, right at the instrument.

Reports on Demand

Results can be reviewed directly from the Viewer and, when needed, converted to professional PDF or editable Excel formats with a single click.

Secure Infrastructure

Viewer communicates securely and one-to-one with the main software's database, preserving data consistency and audit-ready GxP traceability standards.

Fewer Errors, More Trust

By making it structurally impossible to interfere with measurement parameters from the Viewer, you get a monitoring standard your team can trust.
