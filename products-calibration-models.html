Analytical Models

Calibration Models

Pre-built and custom PLS calibration models for NIR spectroscopy — developed, validated, and optimized by our application scientists for immediate deployment on USTECH analyzers.

Model Library by Industry

Our calibration model library covers the most critical analytes across food & feed, dairy, and chemical industries. Each model is built from extensive spectral databases, rigorously validated against reference methods, and optimized for accuracy in real-world process conditions.

● Food & Feed

Grain & Cereals

Moisture, Protein, Fat, Starch, Ash, Gluten, Fiber — for wheat, corn, barley, sorghum, rice, and oats.

Oilseeds & Meals

Protein, Fat (oil), Moisture, Fiber, Urease Activity — for soybeans, rapeseed, sunflower, and their extracted meals.

Compound Feed

Moisture, Crude Protein, Crude Fat, Crude Fiber, Ash, NDF, ADF — for poultry, swine, ruminant, and aqua feeds.

Pet Food

Moisture, Protein, Fat, Fiber, Ash — for dry kibble, wet canned, and extruded treat formulations.

Flour & Milling

Moisture, Protein, Ash, Starch Damage, Wet Gluten — for wheat flour, semolina, and specialty flours.

Meat & Protein

Fat, Moisture, Protein, Collagen — for ground beef, poultry, pork, and processed meat products.

● Dairy

Raw Milk

Butter Fat, Total Protein, Lactose, Total Solids, SNF — for incoming milk receiving and standardization.

Cheese & Whey

Moisture, Fat, Protein, Salt, pH prediction — for cheddar, mozzarella, processed cheese, and whey concentrates.

Milk Powder

Moisture, Protein, Fat, Lactose — for whole milk powder, skim milk powder, and infant formula ingredients.

● Chemical & Pharma

Polymers & Resins

Hydroxyl Value, Acid Number, Moisture, Viscosity prediction — for polyols, alkyd resins, and polyurethane intermediates.

Solvents & Chemicals

Purity, Moisture, Density correlation — for ethanol, methanol, IPA, acetone, and specialty solvents.

Pharma & API

Blend Uniformity, Moisture, API Content, Raw Material Identification — for granulation, tablet, and capsule manufacturing.
