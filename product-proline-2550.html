ProLine2550 FT-NIR Analyzer

The ProLine2550 is USTECH's flagship inline FT-NIR analyzer platform, available in two configurations to match your process complexity and budget. Both models share the same robust industrial design, IP65-rated enclosure, and CE certification — differing in analytical depth and automation capabilities.

Advanced Model Essential Model Compare Models
Flagship Model

ProLine2550 Advanced

Engineered for high-volume bulk production, the Advanced model takes real-time process control to the next level by delivering comprehensive, multi-parameter analysis simultaneously. It integrates seamlessly into key transfer points to provide split-second measurements for challenging materials like powders, granules, pellets, and pastes — capturing all critical constituents at once.

This heavy-duty system features full automation with integrated temperature control and IP65-certified protection, ensuring uninterrupted precision in harsh environments. By delivering complete, real-time data on multiple parameters, the ProLine2550 Advanced allows for immediate process adjustments — maximizing yield, guaranteeing product consistency, and eliminating costly reprocessing.

Cost-Effective Model

ProLine2550 Essential

Designed for straightforward and cost-effective process monitoring, the Essential model delivers real-time, precise measurement for operations with focused analytical needs. Housed in a rugged, industrial-grade enclosure with an IP65 rating, CE certification, and integrated temperature control, the Essential model guarantees stable and long-lasting performance in any production environment. By delivering accurate, actionable data within seconds, it empowers operators to maintain consistent product quality, enhance daily process efficiency, and achieve targeted production standards without complexity.

Software & Automation

ProChem Software & Edge Integration

The ProLine 2550 series is powered by ProChem, our proprietary desktop software and automation interface designed to transform complex spectroscopic data into actionable process insights. Connecting seamlessly via Ethernet, ProChem acts as the central command for both data management and plant automation.

  • ▶ Advanced Data Management: ProChem Desktop offers a user-friendly interface to view, organize, and analyze spectral data in one place.
  • ▶ Chemometric Flexibility: Fully supports material configurations with advanced calibration models created in Eigenvector Solo and PLS, allowing easy adaptation to diverse material types.
  • ▶ Automated & Continuous Scanning: Features an automatic scanning model that drives continuous measurements without requiring manual operator intervention.
  • ▶ Flexible Analytics & Export: Enables deep-dive analytics categorized by specific material or sensor, with seamless data exporting directly to CSV files for easy sharing.
  • ▶ ProChem Automation (PLC Link): Seamlessly bridges the instrument with the plant's PLC through a robust HTTP interface over Ethernet. Product and method names are mapped directly within the interface, enabling efficient remote operation and delivering real-time analytical results back to the PLC for instant process adjustments.
Engineering Details

Hardware Architecture

Built for trouble-free, continuous operation in industrial environments, the ProLine 2550 features premium hardware components engineered for maximum optical sensitivity and physical durability.

  • ▶ High-End FT-NIR Sensor Technology: Powered by an advanced core sensor renowned for its exceptionally wide spectral range at the higher end of the FT-NIR spectrum. This expanded range significantly enhances sensitivity and precision, allowing for the accurate quantification of multiple key parameters in a single scan.
  • ▶ Premium Sapphire Window: Equipped with a 2.5 mm thick sapphire window that provides exceptional mechanical durability against abrasion and harsh process conditions.
  • ▶ Flexible Sample Interface: Features a round sampling head with an adaptable interface designed for easy mounting onto chutes, pipes, or containers.
  • ▶ Industrial Connectivity: Outfitted with a standard 24V DC power connection and reliable Ethernet TCP/IP connectivity, ensuring stable power and rapid data transmission in any plant setup.
Process Intelligence

Continuous Process Optimization

Traditional laboratory grab sampling introduces critical delays and retrospective data, often leading to out-of-specification batches. The ProLine 2550 eliminates these limitations by bringing laboratory-grade FT-NIR accuracy directly into the active production flow. Engineered for continuous inline measurement, it delivers rapid, non-destructive analysis without sampling delays — empowering operators with faster process control and significantly improved product consistency.

Backed by a robust optical design and exceptional measurement stability, the ProLine 2550 ensures reliable performance even in the most demanding industrial environments. Advanced calibration and mathematical transfer capabilities ensure that proprietary prediction models can be efficiently standardized and deployed across different instrument profiles, securing long-term analytical performance.
