Laboratory Milling Device

Tornado+

Water-Cooled High-Speed Performance Precise & Consistent Durable Design Easy-to-Clean

Tornado+ is a high-performance, water-cooled laboratory mill designed for the rapid and uniform grinding of grain, seed, and feed. Its high-speed rotor and precision sieves ensure consistent particle size and reproducible analytical results. The advanced water-cooling system protects sample integrity by preventing heat-induced degradation, while the easy-to-clean design supports intensive daily use in professional environments.

Applications & Value

Versatile Sample Preparation Across the Value Chain

Designed to integrate seamlessly into rigorous quality assurance workflows, the Tornado+ offers flexible sample preparation options to ensure consistency throughout your production cycle.

  • Standardized Raw Material Preparation Rapid and uniform grinding of incoming ingredients, ensuring representative samples for accurate baseline analysis before processing.
  • In-Process Production Support Consistent particle size reduction of intermediate samples to support reliable physical monitoring throughout the manufacturing stages.
  • Final Product Quality Verification Precise sample homogenization for finished goods, ensuring compliance with strict industry standards and internal quality specifications.
  • Universal Matrix Adaptability Capability to process a wide range of materials—including grains, cereals, seeds, feed, and other industrial or laboratory sample matrices—with equal efficiency and precision.
Engineering Details

Benchtop Hardware Architecture

Engineered specifically for reliable, routine laboratory operations, the Tornado+ combines specialized milling mechanics with a robust housing designed to withstand demanding industrial environments.

High-Speed Rotor Mechanism

A powerful motor drives the rotor at speeds of up to 9,000 rpm, ensuring rapid and efficient particle size reduction for diverse sample matrices.

Precision-Engineered Ring Sieves

Equipped with interchangeable ring sieves, seated in a dedicated channel within the grinding chamber, guaranteeing consistent particle geometry and uniform homogeneity across samples.

Integrated Water-Cooling

A dedicated cooling system circulates water at 2 l/min (10–15 °C) to manage frictional heat generated during grinding, protecting the physical integrity of heat-sensitive samples.

Industrial-Grade Chassis

Built with a steel outer casing and an aluminum cover, the housing provides long-term structural durability for intensive daily use.
