ProLine2550 FT-NIR Analyzer

A high-performance process-grade FT-NIR spectrometer engineered for continuous, real-time composition analysis. Leveraging micro-electro-mechanical systems (MEMS) technology and high unit-to-unit similarity, the ProLine2550 provides instant monitoring of moisture, protein, fat, and active ingredients directly inside reactors, pipelines, and rotating blenders.

View Specifications Request a Quote

Continuous Process Optimization

Laboratory grab sampling introduces delay and retrospective data that can lead to out-of-specification batches. The ProLine2550 brings laboratory-grade FT-NIR accuracy directly into the active flow. Featuring a compact, solid-state Michelson interferometer design with zero moving parts, it delivers unmatched reliability, signal-to-noise ratio, and wavelength accuracy.

Thanks to its outstanding unit-to-unit similarity, calibration models developed on one ProLine2550 instrument can be directly transferred to other units with zero adjustments. This unique plug-and-play capability drastically reduces setup time, calibration efforts, and lifetime cost of ownership.

Key Value Drivers

  • ? Wireless & Battery-Powered Probes: Option for battery-powered reflectance probes, ideal for mounting directly on rotating bin blenders without wire entanglement.
  • ? High Unit-to-Unit Similarity: Match profiles perfectly across multiple instruments, allowing seamless calibration transfer without instrument-specific adjustments.
  • ? Rugged & Cleanable: Crevice-free sanitary sapphire windows designed to withstand daily CIP steam cleaning, high pressures, and corrosive chemical processes.

Technical Data

Specifications

  • Optical Configuration MEMS-based Michelson interferometer (FT-NIR)
  • Spectral Range 800 to 2600 nm (12,500 to 3,850 cm?—)
  • Spectral Resolution 8 cm?— (adjustable)
  • Signal-to-Noise Ratio > 25,000:1 (typical)
  • Measurement Rate < 1 second per full scan
  • Enclosure Rating 316L Stainless Steel, electro-polished (IP65 / IP69K washdown rated)
  • Optical Interface Fiber-optic connection, sanitary weld-in probes, or wireless diffuse reflectance
  • Window Material Sapphire crystal (high temperature, pressure, and scratch resistant)
  • Connectivity Protocols OPC UA, Modbus TCP, Analog 4-20mA, Profinet PLC integration
  • Power Requirements 24 V DC or 100-240 V AC, 50/60 Hz
  • Reference Standards Integrated internal wavelength calibration reference
