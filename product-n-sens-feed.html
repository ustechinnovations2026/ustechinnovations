At-Line FT-NIR Spectrometer

USTECH MasterLine

The MasterLine FT-NIR Analyzer is a high-performance, compact benchtop analytical instrument engineered for the rapid, precise, and non-destructive analysis of raw materials, animal feed, and forage. Operating via diffuse reflectance methodology across the near-infrared spectrum, it eliminates traditional laboratory delays by delivering accurate quality control insights directly within production facilities and lab environments.

Designed to withstand real-world industrial variables—including dust, moisture, ambient vibrations, and temperature fluctuations—the MasterLine empowers manufacturers to optimize production, minimize raw material costs, and consistently manufacture closer to target specifications with total confidence.

Versatile Quality Monitoring Across the Value Chain

  • Incoming Raw Material Inspection Fast, non-destructive screening of incoming ingredients at sample reception to verify quality before unloading.

Engineering Details

Benchtop Hardware Architecture

Engineered specifically for reliable, routine laboratory and near-line testing, the MasterLine combines specialized sample handling mechanics with a rugged housing designed to withstand demanding industrial environments.

Automated Motorized Cover

Features a smart, automated open-and-close lid mechanism controllable via both the integrated physical button and the software interface. This design seals the analysis chamber completely during scanning, protecting the optical path from ambient light interference while ensuring maximum operator safety against pinch risks.

Dynamic Rotating Chamber

Equipped with a built-in rotating dish mechanism that spins the sample cup continuously during the analysis cycle. This dynamic movement ensures comprehensive surface scanning, delivering highly representative and repeatable data for unground grains, pellets, and non-homogeneous feed ingredients.

Aluminium Housing

Encased in a robust, precision-machined aluminium chassis that shields the delicate optical components from operational variables such as dust, moisture, and micro-vibrations, ensuring long-term hardware durability.

Software & Control

Intelligent Software Control via ProChem

The MasterLine operates seamlessly through ProChem, a proprietary control desktop application that transforms complex near-infrared data into clean, actionable analytical reports. Designed with modern laboratory workflows in mind, ProChem streamlines device management and data logging through a highly intuitive digital interface.

  • Secure User Access Features a secure user authentication system requiring a specific username and password, protecting proprietary calibration methods and historical analysis records from unauthorized access.
  • Streamlined Method & Analysis Workflow Operators can instantly select predefined chemometric calibration methods from the "Analysis" tab. Once triggered, the software displays a real-time countdown timer while synchronized sample dish rotation takes place, projecting comprehensive multi-parameter results onto the screen immediately upon completion.
  • Automated Hardware Commands Equipped with integrated software buttons that allow direct, manual or automated control of the motorized chamber cover, removing the need for manual structural handling.
Technical Data

Technical Specifications & Installation Requirements

Review the detailed engineering parameters, electrical limits, and environmental operating conditions of the MasterLine series to ensure seamless deployment within your facility or laboratory.

Operational & Optical Specifications

  • Measurement Mode Solid / Liquid (Powders, unground grains, pellets, liquids, suspensions)
  • Wavelength Range 950 – 1650 nm (10,526 – 6,060 cm-1)
  • Reference Standard Spectralon (>99% Diffuse Reflectance)
  • Absorbance Range Up to 3 AU
  • Analysis Time < 30 seconds (Adjustable)
  • Analyzed Parameters Protein, Moisture, Oil/Fat, Ash, Starch, and Cellulose (Customizable)

Electrical, Mechanical & Environmental Requirements

  • Operating Temperature 0°C to +35°C (Relative Humidity ≤ 85% at 35°C)
  • Storage & Transit Temp. -20°C to +40°C (Relative Humidity ≤ 85% at 40°C)
  • Data Connectivity USB 2.0 Type A-B
  • Power Adaptor Interface 2.1 Plug-in
  • Input Voltage 176 – 264 V AC (50-60 Hz)
  • Output Voltage / Current 24 V DC / 3.6 A
  • Dimensions (W x H x D) 250 mm x 355 mm x 170 mm
  • Housing Material Precision-Machined Industrial Aluminium
